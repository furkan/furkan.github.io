Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Odak handles information. Odak is a focus timer app for macOS designed to store your productivity data on your device.

Information Odak Stores

Odak may store the following information locally on your Mac:

  • Tasks you create in the app.
  • Focus session history, including session duration, date, and task name.
  • Timer settings, notification settings, and custom reminder messages.

How Information Is Used

This information is used only to provide app features such as timers, task tracking, reports, charts, reminders, and import/export.

Data Sharing

Odak does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or cross-app tracking.

Network Use

Odak is designed to work locally. It does not upload your tasks, session history, settings, or reminder messages to a server operated by the developer.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Odak uses macOS notifications to alert you when a focus or break session ends. Notification permission is managed by macOS and can be changed in System Settings.

Import and Export

Odak lets you export and import your task and session history as a JSON file. Exported files are saved only where you choose to save them. You are responsible for how you store, share, or delete exported files.

Data Deletion

You can delete tasks and session history in the app. You can also remove locally stored app data by deleting the app and its associated data from your Mac.

Children's Privacy

Odak is not directed to children under 13, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if Odak's features or data practices change. The latest version will be posted at this page.
